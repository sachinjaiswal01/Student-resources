Elizabeth Olsen is an American actress best known for her roles in the films "Martha Marcy May Marlene" (2011), "The Avengers" (2012), and "Avengers: Infinity War" (2018).

She is the younger sister of actresses and fashion designers Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen. Elizabeth Olsen began her acting career on Broadway, making her debut in the 2008 play "Impressionism" opposite Jeremy Irons. She has since appeared in a number of films and television shows, including "Godzilla" (2014), "Captain America: Civil War" (2016), and "Sorry for Your Loss" (2018-2020). In addition to her acting work, Olsen is also known for her philanthropy and support of various charitable causes.
